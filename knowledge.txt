OVERHEAT: low coolant, radiator blockage, fan failure, thermostat stuck

MISFIRE: worn spark plugs, injector fault, low compression

BATTERY: old battery, alternator failure, loose terminals

BRAKES: worn pads, damaged rotors, lack of lubrication

LOW_FUEL_EFFICIENCY: clogged air filter, bad spark plugs, low tire pressure

ENGINE_NOISE: low oil level, worn bearings, timing issue
